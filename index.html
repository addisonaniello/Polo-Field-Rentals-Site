Welcome to Your Hudson Valley Retreat

Charming & Spacious

Escape to our beautifully appointed vacation rentals nestled on private acres directly across from the prestigious Mashomack Polo Fields. Perfect for families or groups seeking a tranquil getaway with easy access to Hudson Valley attractions.

Unlike traditional hotels or B&Bs in Pine Plains, our vacation rentals offer complete privacy, full kitchens, and more space for families. Compare our rates with local hotels and bed and breakfasts - you'll get more for your money!

The open-concept living areas flow seamlessly into gourmet kitchens stocked with everything you need to prepare memorable meals. Cozy up by the fireplace or head outside to enjoy the expansive yards, fire pits, and al fresco dining.

Endless Activities

Summer: Watch polo matches from your front porch, enjoy local farm stands and wineries.

Fall: Experience stunning foliage, apple picking, and pumpkin patches.

Winter: Hit the slopes at nearby ski resorts or enjoy cozy nights by the fire.

Spring: Hike Stissing Mountain or explore blooming gardens throughout the valley.

Why Choose Our Rentals? Pet-friendly, EV charging, game rooms, and direct polo field views you won't find at local inns.
