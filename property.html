Welcome to Your Hudson Valley Retreat

Charming & Spacious

Escape to this beautifully appointed 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom home nestled on 4 private acres directly across from the prestigious Mashomack Polo Fields. Perfect for families or groups seeking a tranquil getaway with easy access to Hudson Valley attractions.

Unlike traditional hotels or B&Bs in Pine Plains, our vacation rental offers complete privacy, full kitchen, and more space for families. Compare our rates with local hotels and bed and breakfasts - you'll get more for your money!

The open-concept living area flows seamlessly into a gourmet kitchen stocked with everything you need to prepare memorable meals. Cozy up by the fireplace or head outside to enjoy the expansive yard, fire pit, and al fresco dining on the patio.

Summer: Watch polo matches from your front porch, enjoy local farm stands and wineries.

Fall: Experience stunning foliage, apple picking, and pumpkin patches.

Winter: Hit the slopes at nearby ski resorts or enjoy cozy nights by the fire.
